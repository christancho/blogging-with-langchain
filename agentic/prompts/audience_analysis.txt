   - What they already know vs. what they need to learn

   - What outcome do they want after reading this article?

4. **Content Angle Recommendation**
   - What angle will resonate most with this reader?
   - What tone will build trust (e.g., peer-to-peer expert, mentor, practical guide)?
   - What should the article emphasize vs. skip based on this audience?

5. **Engagement Hooks**
   - What opening scenario would make this reader think "that's me"?
   - What specific examples from their world would resonate?
   - What common misconceptions can we challenge to grab attention?
